Who We Are.

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is a premier industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to building the next generation of risk professionals.

We focus on digital, cyber, and technology risk roles within regulated, high-consequence environments. Our unique model emphasizes the critical link between rigorous academic insight—fostered through strategic UK university partnerships—and practical, real-world project execution.
